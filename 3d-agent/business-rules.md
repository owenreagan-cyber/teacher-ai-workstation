# Business Rules

Business designs should be original or properly licensed.

## Track for each product

- Product name
- Version
- Purpose
- Target customer
- Use mode: personal, classroom, business/commercial, repair/replacement, or reference-only
- Material
- Print time
- Estimated cost
- Selling notes
- License/source
- Commercial review notes
- Partner notes

## Separation rules

- Keep business products separate from personal, classroom, and reference experiments.
- Do not assume a downloaded/reference model is commercially usable.
- Keep product decisions inspectable.
- Recommend human review before marking anything commercial-ready.
- The agent may recommend review before sale, but it cannot block sale decisions or printing decisions.
