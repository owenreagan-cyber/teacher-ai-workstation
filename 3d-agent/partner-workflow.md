# Partner Workflow

Owen and his partner share the MacBook for 3D business work. Commercial product decisions should be inspectable.

Product review should include design review and commercial/IP review. Track who requested the design, who reviewed it, and whether it is personal, classroom, business/commercial, repair/replacement, or reference-only.

## Both partners should review

- New commercial product
- Design based on existing/reference item
- Product listing copy
- Safety-sensitive item
- Customer/client request
- Major revision to an existing product

## Partner review template

```markdown
## Partner review

- Product/design name:
- Use mode:
- Requested by:
- Reviewed by:
- Commercial reviewed: yes/no
- IP/license reviewed: yes/no
- Safety reviewed: yes/no
- Notes:
- Next action:
```

The review process is advisory and organizational. It does not block printing. The human operator makes the final print decision.
