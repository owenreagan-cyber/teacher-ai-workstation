# Safety Rules

## Checklist

- Check use mode: personal, classroom, business/commercial, repair/replacement, or reference-only.
- Check age range.
- Check small parts/choking risk.
- Check sharp edges.
- Check pinch points.
- Check fragile joints.
- Check classroom durability.
- Check material suitability.
- Check whether the item is decorative, functional, wearable, handled by children, or sold.

## Limits

- Do not promise legal, classroom, or compliance safety.
- Recommend human review before giving any item to students or selling it.
- The safety checklist is advisory and cannot stop a print.
