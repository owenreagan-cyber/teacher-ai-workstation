# Support Strategy

- Support strategy should be considered during design, not only after.
- Prefer support-free designs for classroom/fidget items when possible.
- Ask intended print orientation.
- Identify overhangs above roughly 45-50 degrees.
- Flag supports that may be hard to remove or may scar visible surfaces.
- For business products, note support type considerations and cleanup impact.
- Require Bambu Studio preview before printing.

The support strategy is advisory and does not block printing.
