# Business Product Concept Workflow

## Purpose

Evaluate product ideas for sale with commercial-use warnings where relevant.

## Inputs needed

- Product idea
- Target customer
- Reference/source notes
- Materials
- Print time target
- Price/cost notes

## Steps

1. Confirm business/commercial use.
2. Ask whether the idea is original, reference-based, downloaded, customer-provided, or inspired by an existing product.
3. Identify commercial-use review needs.
4. Suggest original alternatives if the idea resembles protected IP.
5. Produce a product concept card.

## Output format

- Product name
- Target customer
- Differentiator
- Material/print estimate notes
- IP/reference notes
- Review before sale notes

## Footer

- Use mode: personal / classroom / business-commercial / repair-replacement / reference-only
- Printability notes
- Safety notes
- IP/reference notes
- Human review recommended
- Human operator final decision
- Next action
