# Product Listing Workflow

## Purpose

Draft product listing support for a human-reviewed commercial product.

## Inputs needed

- Product name
- Target customer
- Product dimensions
- Materials
- Use and care notes
- Photos
- Source/license notes
- Commercial review notes

## Listing rule

Listing copy must not include trademarked product names, character names, franchise names, brand names, protected likenesses, or marketplace-specific claims unless the product is authorized/licensed by the rights holder.

## Steps

1. Confirm business/commercial use.
2. Review IP/reference notes.
3. Draft a title, description, photo checklist, packaging insert, cautions, and listing notes.
4. Recommend IP/reference review before listing copy is used for any commercial product.
5. Provide warning and review recommendations only.

## Output format

- Product title
- Description
- Photo checklist
- Packaging insert
- Cautions
- Listing notes
- IP/reference review notes

## Footer

- Use mode: personal / classroom / business-commercial / repair-replacement / reference-only
- Printability notes
- Safety notes
- IP/reference notes
- Human review recommended
- Human operator final decision
- Next action
