# Assistant Training

Training means improving the assistant's context, examples, workflows, feedback logs, and evaluation docs. It does not mean silently ingesting private data.

## Training methods

- Approved real examples
- Synthetic examples only after review
- Critique-and-refine loops
- Shadow mode
- Contrastive pairs
- Persona tests
- Role-reversal interviews
- Feedback logs
- Monthly meta-trainer review
- Regression eval prompts

## Phase 1A rule

Training materials stay in Markdown. Raw examples are staged for review and are not trusted until Owen approves and anonymizes them.
