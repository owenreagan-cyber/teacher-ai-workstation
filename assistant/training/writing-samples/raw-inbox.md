# Raw Writing Sample Inbox

WARNING: This file must never be automatically loaded as assistant context. It is a staging area only. Content moves to approved-samples.md only after manual human review.

## Safety rules

- Anonymize student and parent names.
- Do not include grades, IEPs, discipline details, or private contact info.
- Do not paste confidential staff/admin documents.
- Approved examples are the only ones used for writing style.
