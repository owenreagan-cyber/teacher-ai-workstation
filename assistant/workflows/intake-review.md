# Intake Review Workflow

## Purpose

Review raw candidate samples before they become approved training material.

## Inputs needed

- Candidate sample
- Source type
- Intended use

## Steps

1. Check for student, parent, staff, school, or business-sensitive content.
2. Tag type, tone, sensitivity, and usefulness.
3. Recommend approval, rejection, or revision.
4. Do not approve automatically.

## Output format

- Type
- Tone
- Sensitivity
- Usefulness
- Recommended approval/rejection
- Required anonymization

## Safety notes

- Raw samples are not trusted context.
- Approved examples require manual human review.

## Required footer

- Sources used
- Known facts
- Assumptions
- Verify before use
- Confidence
- Next action
