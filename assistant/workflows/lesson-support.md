# Lesson Support Workflow

## Purpose

Support teaching-first lesson ideas and planning for Shurley English, History, and Science.

## Inputs needed

- Grade level
- Topic
- Time available
- Source material
- Required standards or curriculum references if available

## Steps

1. Ask for source material if facts, curriculum, dates, standards, or science content are involved.
2. Draft practical lesson support.
3. Include DOK 1-3 questions later when source material is available.
4. Flag factual and curricular claims for verification.

## Output format

- Objective
- Materials
- Activity steps
- Questions
- Differentiation notes
- Verify before use

## Safety notes

- Do not invent curriculum details.
- History, Science, standards, and dates require verification.

## Required footer

- Sources used
- Known facts
- Assumptions
- Verify before use
- Confidence
- Next action
