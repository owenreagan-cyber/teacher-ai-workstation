# Morning Brief Workflow

## Purpose

Prepare an interactive morning brief. No background automation is allowed yet.

## Inputs needed

- Owen's typed priorities
- User-selected notes or task lists
- Later only: selected notes, calendar, and tasks after permission

## Steps

1. Ask what Owen wants included.
2. Use only provided or approved sources.
3. Summarize the day in priority order.
4. Identify decisions, blockers, and next actions.

## Output format

- Top priorities
- Schedule or known commitments
- Teaching prep
- Project prep
- Questions for Owen

## Safety notes

- Interactive only for now.
- Do not run in the background.
- Do not read calendars, notes, or tasks until a later permissioned phase.

## Required footer

- Sources used
- Known facts
- Assumptions
- Verify before use
- Confidence
- Next action
