# 3D Printing Roadmap

## Phase 3D-0: Readiness

Readiness docs, folders, Bambu Studio, OpenSCAD, verification checklists.

## Phase 3D-1: Manual design brief assistant

Turn ideas into structured design briefs.

## Phase 3D-2: OpenSCAD generation with test suite

Generate small parametric models and run the OpenSCAD regression suite.

## Phase 3D-3: STL/export/preview loop

Export and preview files manually.

## Phase 3D-4: Bambu Studio verification workflow

Check scale, supports, colors, printer profile, layer preview, and print estimate.

## Phase 3D-5: Print QA and failure diagnosis

Use prototype logs and failure diagnosis workflows.

## Phase 3D-6: Business product catalog

Track product cards, versions, materials, cost notes, listing notes, and review notes.

## Phase 3D-7: Partner workflow and product review

Add partner review for commercial products, reference-based designs, listings, safety-sensitive items, customer/client requests, and major revisions.

## Phase 3D-8: Optional slicer/printer automation

Optional slicer/printer automation only after safety gates and human review routines are proven.

## Lanes

- Teaching/classroom lane
- Business/product lane
- Personal/reference lane
- Copyright/IP/reference lane
- Verification/training lane

## Advisory-only principle

The agent can warn and classify, but cannot block or stop printing. The human operator makes the final print decision.
